Sports Awards

National Sports Awards

  • Khel Ratna Award: Recognizes exceptional performances in sports over a four-year period.
  • Arjuna Award: Given for consistent excellence over four years.
  • Dronacharya Award: Recognizes coaches producing medal-winning athletes at international events.
  • Dhyan Chand Award: Honors individuals contributing significantly to sports promotion.
  • Maulana Abul Kalam Azad Trophy: Recognizes top performances in inter-university tournaments
  • Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puruskar: Acknowledges entities promoting sports development over three years. Includes organizations, schools, and individuals. Encourages sports initiatives.
